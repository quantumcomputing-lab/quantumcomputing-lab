IEEE QCE 2022

IEEE's flagship quantum computing conference just concluded at Denver, Colorado (https://qce.quantum.ieee.org/2022/). It was attended by researchers, academics, industrialists, VCs, policy makers and also government representatives with interest in the coming quantum revolution. I had three takeaways from this conference: 1) The eco-system is growing exponentially across the value chain without waiting for fault-tolerant error correction 2) A stronger focus is required on reliability and reproducibility of claims on "breakthroughs" 3) Investors might have a need for good translators who can strike the balance between hype and rational optimism and explain their reasoning with clarity and conviction
